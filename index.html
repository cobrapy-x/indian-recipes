🩺 Diabetic Diet Tips

  • Choose brown rice or hand-pounded rice over white rice
  • Prefer whole wheat roti over maida
  • Eat smaller portions more frequently
  • Fish (especially hilsa, rohu, catla) is excellent for diabetics
  • Dal provides slow-release protein and fibre
  • Avoid frying — steam, boil or shallow fry
  • Mustard oil (Bengali cooking) is heart-healthy
